Deadlift

Start with the barbell placed on the floor. Place your feet under the bar shoulder-width apart. With a straight back, bend your knees, using both hands to grip the bar, and keeping it close to your body. Pull the bar upwards while keeping your arms straight. Bring your shoulders back while you pull the barbell and stand up. After pulling the bar up to your thighs, gently squat back down until the bar is back on the floor to complete one rep.

Squat

Postion yourself standing with feet shoulder-width apart. Move under the barebell and slowly lift it onto your shoulders keeping your back straight. Place the barbell onto the back of your shoulers gently while holding both sides with adequate balance. Slowly squat down, bend your knees until your thighs are parallel with the floor. Slowly rise up again to the starting position to complete one rep.
