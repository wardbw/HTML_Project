Smoothie Recipes

Smoothies are the easiest and fastest way to pack on muscle. All you need to do is toss in all the ingredients and blend!

Early Morning Shake (1k calories)

  1. 1 vanilla Boost Plus
  2. 1/2 cup of whole milk
  3. 1 tbsp coconut oil
  4. 1 tbsp almond butter
  5. 1 tbsp greek yogurt
  6. 1/2 cup raw pre-ground oats
  7. 1 banana
  8. 1 tsp ground flaxseed

Quick High Calorie Shake (1k calories)

  1. 1/2 cup oats
  2. 1/2 cup whole milk
  3. 1/8 cup peanut butter
  4. 1 scoop chocolate flavored whey protein
  5. 1 tb honey
  6. 1 tb nutella
  7. 1 banana

"Taste like icecream" shake (550 calories)

  1. 4 oz papaya
  2. 1 banana
  3. 1/2 cup oats
  4. 1 and 1/2 cup milk
  5. 1 cup ice
  6. 1 scoop strawberry whey protein

The 3k breakfast shake (3000k calories!)

  1. 1 cup oats
  2. 6oz greek yogurt
  3. 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. 2 tbsp peanut butter
  5. creatine
  6. 2 scoop weight gainer
  7. 2 cups whole milk
  8. 1 cup raw almonds
