Overhead Press

Stand with the bar on your front shoulders gripping the bar strongly. Lift your chest while arching your upper back. Hold the bar and press it in a vertical line, locking your shoulders while pressing it over the head. Stay close to the bar while you press the weight up, while slowly shifting your torso forward once the bar has passed your forehead. Hold the bar over your shoulders, without bending your legs for proper balance.
