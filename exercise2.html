Barbell Rows

The barbell row starts with the bar on the floor. Bend over and grab the bar, using medium grip width with your hands and palms facing down. Keeping your hips higher and bending your knees in such a way that the bar doesn't hit them, hold the bar and slowly pull the bar against your lower chest, keeping the torso horizontal. Place the bar back on the floor to complete one rep.

Bench Press

Lie down on the bench with a flat back, placing your feet on the ground. Hold the barbell with your arms a little wider than should-width apart. Slowly lift the bar off the rack and gradually bring it to the chest. Push the bar upwards keeping the pressure on the chest muscles. Once you are back at the top of the movement, you have completed one rep.
